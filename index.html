Come in and get a workout

We offer our community the best in boxing and fitness training, physically and mentally. Donaire Sr. Boxing is established to provide a place where one can learn boxing and enjoy the experience while making the commitment to learn and improve one's self-esteem, concentration, discipline and self-control.

We are dedicated to provide amateur and professional boxers the highest quality boxing instruction available, in a safe, well supervised and positive training environment so that boxers of all level can progress quickly and confidently toward their personal goals. We believe in the value and importance of exercises and healthy living. We are committed to provide the community a variety of fitness boxing classes.
